Service 02a

Project Management as a Service

Don't need full construction management? We also take on project management on its own, running the schedule, contractors, and budget for a project you've already started, plus the equipment to keep it moving.

Project management scope

  • Schedule planning & milestone tracking
  • Contractor & subcontractor coordination
  • Budget tracking & cost reporting
  • Procurement of materials & equipment
  • Progress reporting to the client
  • Rescue & oversight of stalled projects

Construction equipment hire

  • Excavators (mini & standard)
  • Backhoe loaders & graders
  • Tipper / dumper trucks
  • Concrete mixer trucks
  • Road rollers & compactors
  • Generator sets for site power

Equipment can be hired with an operator or self-drive, alongside our vehicle fleet or as a standalone request. Let us know on the contact form.
